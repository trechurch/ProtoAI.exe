Recall@K:
Fraction of all relevant items retrieved in the top K results.
Formula: (Relevant items found in top K) / (Total relevant items)

Precision@K:
Fraction of relevant items among the top K retrieved results.
Formula: (Number of relevant items in top K) / K

NDCG (Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain):
Measures ranking quality with position discounting.
Higher relevance items should rank higher.
Normalized against ideal ranking (0-1).

DCG Calculation:
DCG@K = Σ (rel_i / log₂(i + 1)) for i=1 to K

rel_i: relevance score of item at position i
Higher relevance at top positions weighted more.

MAP (Mean Average Precision):
Averages Average Precision across all queries.
Emphasizes ranking quality over multiple retrievals.
Better for overall system performance than single Precision@K.

MAP vs Kendall's Tau:

MAP: Precision-focused, rewards relevant items in top ranks across queries.
Kendall's Tau: Pure ranking correlation (concordant/discordant pairs). Measures order agreement, ignores absolute relevance scores.

MAP vs NDCG:

MAP: Binary relevance, no position discount, query-averaged.
NDCG: Graded relevance, position discounting (log), normalized to ideal.

NDCG vs Precision@K:

Precision@K: Fraction of relevant items in top K. Ignores order within K and graded relevance.
NDCG: Position-discounted graded relevance. Rewards higher relevance at top ranks.

NDCG better for nuanced, position-sensitive ranking  and ranking quality.
NDCG for retrieval ranking; ROC AUC for binary detection.
