### 🛠️ Best DIY Websites for Every Maker

| Resource             | Focus Area & Highlights                                                                 |
|----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Craftsy](https://www.craftsy.com)         | 🎨 Crafting, sewing, painting, and baking tutorials. Offers free and paid video courses. Great for skill-building. |
| [Instructables](https://www.instructables.com)   | 🔧 Community-driven platform with step-by-step guides for electronics, woodworking, crafts, and more. Ideal for tinkerers. |
| [Make:](https://makezine.com)              | 🤖 Tech-centric DIY projects, including robotics, wearables, and Raspberry Pi builds. Also hosts Maker Faires. |
| [Apartment Therapy](https://www.apartmenttherapy.com) | 🏠 Home improvement, décor hacks, and small-space design tips. Stylish, budget-friendly ideas. |
| [DIY Projects](https://diyprojects.com)    | 🧰 Broad DIY coverage—crafts, home hacks, survival gear, and seasonal projects. Great for browsing inspiration. |

---

### 🌿 Bonus Picks for Niche DIY Passions

- **[Ana White](https://www.ana-white.com)**: Furniture plans and woodworking tutorials with clear diagrams and community builds.
- **[Crafting a Green World](https://craftingagreenworld.com)**: Eco-conscious DIY with upcycling, mending, and sustainable materials.
- **[The Family Handyman](https://www.familyhandyman.com)**: Practical home maintenance and repair guides, perfect for weekend warriors.
- **[Brit + Co](https://www.brit.co)**: Colorful, trendy DIYs—from jewelry to party décor—with a strong visual aesthetic.
